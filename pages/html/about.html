About Kris Yotam

Welcome to my platform! I’m Kris Yotam, a passionate advocate of Israelite apologetics. This space is dedicated to exploring and understanding the deep-rooted truths of faith, history, and scripture.

My Journey

I began my journey seeking to understand the deep roots of faith and history. Through years of research, reflection, and spiritual growth, I embraced Israelite heritage as the foundation of true Christian belief.

Mission

My mission is to provide a platform for exploring Israelite apologetics and Christian teachings. I aim to highlight the relevance of ancient truths in a modern world, sharing insights that foster a deeper understanding of scripture and history.

Beliefs

I believe in the importance of both the law and grace in understanding God's will. My work emphasizes the balance between biblical law and New Testament grace, grounded in the historical Israelite context of scripture.

Publications

I have contributed to several articles and publications focusing on the intersection of Israelite traditions and Christian theology. These works aim to clarify misconceptions and bring to light the profound truths embedded in scripture.

Contact

Feel free to get in touch with me! Whether you’re interested in discussing apologetics or have any questions, I’m happy to connect. Reach me at contact@krisyotam.com.
